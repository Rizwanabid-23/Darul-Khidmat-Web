Our First Step
So today was the first day we step out for the HOPEWITHDUK. We had mixed emotions. We were happy , worried and tensed at the same time. We had money but we didn't know where to spend it. Alhumdulillah we collected almost Rs.20,000/- within three weeks and its a victory for all of the people who are a part of it. We were sitting and thinking where should the money be spent.

Then we hit upon this idea. It was 41'C and we just went on executing this idea without any single second thought. May Allah accept this from us. We have helped the people and we shall inform you people because HOPEWITHDUK IS BECAUSE OF EACH OF YOU PEOPLE.

We need you! The people need you! A dua can change your life and I MEAN IT.

A daily 10 rupee note will not make you poor. We never know what is good for us. The negativity will stop you ke bhae kya kro ge ye sab karke kya hi miljaega sari duniya kar rhi he tumhare karna se kya hojaega (Translation: What will you get doing this? Everyone is doing this. What difference will you make?) and when you say to yourself “WHY NOT ME?! I HAVE TO PLAY A PART” this is your victory. Regards❤️
