Our First Step
So today was the first day we step out for the HOPEWITHDUK. We had mixed emotions. We were happy , worried and tensed at the same time. We had money but we didn't know where to spend it. Alhumdulillah we collected almost Rs.20,000/- within three weeks and its a victory for all of the people who are a part of it. We were sitting and thinking where should the money be spent.

Then we hit upon this idea. It was 41'C and we just went on executing this idea without any single second thought. May Allah accept this from us. We have helped the people and we shall inform you people because HOPEWITHDUK IS BECAUSE OF EACH OF YOU PEOPLE.

We need you! The people need you! A dua can change your life and I MEAN IT.

A daily 10 rupee note will not make you poor. We never know what is good for us. The negativity will stop you ke bhae kya kro ge ye sab karke kya hi miljaega sari duniya kar rhi he tumhare karna se kya hojaega (Translation: What will you get doing this? Everyone is doing this. What difference will you make?) and when you say to yourself “WHY NOT ME?! I HAVE TO PLAY A PART” this is your victory. Regards❤️

Flood Relief campaign
In August 2022, Pakistan faced its worst flood situation ever with 1/3 of the country under water. Over 1200 died and millions of people were displaced. In these crisis, HopewithDUK-a student based charity organization, started working with the idea of helping people without the discrimination of colour, caste and religion. We work for welfare of humanity because it's the beauty of our religion.

During this event, we contacted a family of Nawab shah. We came to know about them by our friend. We contacted them and came to know that conditions there are just horrible. "I was talking to one of the families belonging to Nawab shah . Wedera(chief) of those areas are not providing the people with the things they have promised. They also stopped people to ask for help from any other sources. People are injured and terrified. The people are just lying and requesting people to take their families to Nothern Punjab", our co-founder told the team.
Instead of remaining idle, we had to step up and provide all the help we could. It is our moral obligation to sacrifice all we have in the name of Allah. Our team members are contributing as much as they can. One of our family friends are taking 3 trucks of ration to Tonsa and Rajanpur with the help of Jazba e Noo
We urge you to verify the sources you are contributing to. It is the test of our faith. Even your little contribution will have an effect. So don't wait and help Pakistani brothers and sisters in this hour of need.
